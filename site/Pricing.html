Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

yes. swap plans by changing your wallet config. no lock-up, no penalty. a reserved pool can be drained back to your wallet anytime.

How is the markup calculated?

a small percent on top of the provider's quote, shown before you sign. no monthly fee. you stop calling, you stop paying.

What does my wallet need?

a standard solana wallet (phantom, solflare, hardware, custom signer). hold usdc to spend and a little sol for gas. that's it.

Is the protocol open?

yes. the spec is mit-licensed, the settlement contracts are deployed on solana mainnet and publicly inspectable. wallets stay non-custodial.
